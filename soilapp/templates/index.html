About Precision Agriculture

Precision agriculture is a modern farming practice that uses technology to optimize field-level management regarding crop farming.

It helps in increasing efficiency, saving costs, and promoting environmental sustainability.

Technologies used

GPS/GNSS: Precision mapping and navigation.

Drones: Aerial imagery for crop monitoring.

IoT Sensors: Real-time data on soil and weather conditions.

Data Analytics: Transforming data into actionable insights.

Applications

Crop Management: Optimizing planting and fertilization.

Soil Management: Monitoring soil health and moisture.

Pest Control: Identifying and managing pest outbreaks.
